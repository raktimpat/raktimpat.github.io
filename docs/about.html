About Me

Hi, I'm Raktim — a Data Scientist who enjoys turning ideas into things that work. I love exploring how small experiments grow into reliable systems that make an impact. What drives me most is the mix of logic, creativity, and persistence it takes to bring something to life — whether it's a model, a workflow, or just a personal project.

But my world isn't just made of data and code. I've always been drawn to the outdoors — biking is something I can't stay away from for long, and nothing clears my head better than a mountain trip. The rhythm of the road and the calm of high places keep me grounded and remind me why balance matters.

Sports have been a big part of who I am too. I've been an NCC cadet and had the chance to attend the RDC camp, an experience that taught me teamwork, leadership, and a lot about staying calm under pressure. Back in college, I also coordinated a sports fest, managing schedules, people, and more than a few surprises — and learned how collaboration can turn chaos into something memorable.

When I'm off the field or away from my desk, you'll probably find me sketching, watching esports, or diving into a good fantasy series or anime. I've always been a sucker for imaginative worlds — stories where creativity and curiosity drive everything forward. In a way, that's what I love most about data science too: finding patterns in complexity and building something meaningful out of it.
